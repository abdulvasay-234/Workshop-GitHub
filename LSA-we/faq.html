About Lords Skill Academy

Lords Skill Academy (LSA) is an industry-aligned training institution focused on skill development and career preparation. We offer specialized courses in programming, data science, and other tech domains. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, preparing students for real-world careers.

Unlike traditional colleges, LSA focuses on skill-first education with industry-aligned curriculum. We emphasize practical learning, hands-on projects, and career guidance. Our courses are designed to be flexible, allowing you to learn at your own pace while getting personalized support. We also provide career assistance and job referrals after course completion.

Yes, Lords Skill Academy is affiliated with Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology (LIET), a well-established educational institution. This affiliation ensures quality standards, infrastructure, and professional guidance.

Eligibility & Background

Career Support & Placements

Yes, we provide comprehensive career support and placement assistance. This includes resume building, interview coaching, portfolio reviews, and job referrals. Our career advisors work with you to identify opportunities that match your skills and interests.

We do not guarantee job placements, but we do our best to support your career journey. Job outcomes depend on several factors including your performance in the course, effort in learning, interview skills, and market conditions. We provide tools and guidance, but the effort is a joint responsibility.

Job roles depend on your chosen course. For example, Data Science students may pursue roles as data analysts or junior data scientists. Programming students can explore roles in web development, software engineering, or backend development. Our career advisors help match your skills with relevant opportunities.

Yes! Career support doesn't end when your course finishes. We continue to provide mentoring, job leads, and guidance even after graduation. Our alumni community is active, and you'll have access to networking opportunities and ongoing learning resources.

Fees, Duration & Certification

Course fees vary depending on the program, duration, and features. We offer affordable pricing and flexible payment plans to make quality education accessible. For specific fee information, please contact us or visit the course details page.

Yes, we understand financial constraints. LSA offers flexible payment options including EMI (monthly installments), upfront payment discounts, and scholarships for deserving students. Payment terms are discussed during enrollment.

Yes, upon successful completion of the course, you'll receive an LSA certificate of completion. This certificate is recognized in the industry and demonstrates your competency. Your certificate is added to your professional profile on our platform.

Our refund policy is fair and transparent. Refunds (if eligible) are provided within a specified timeframe of enrollment, subject to our terms. Specific refund terms are communicated at enrollment. For details, please review our Terms & Conditions.
